üéÆ Normal Mode - How to Play

  1. Choose Your Letters: Each player selects either the starting or ending letter
  2. Race to Find a Word: Both players compete to type a valid English word first
  3. First Valid Word Wins: The first player to submit a correct word wins the round
  4. Win the Game: Be the first to win the required number of rounds!

‚öîÔ∏è Battle Royale Mode - How to Play

  1. Choose Your Letters: Same as Normal mode - select starting and ending letters
  2. Creator Goes First: The game creator must submit the first valid word
  3. Timer Resets: After each valid word, the timer resets to full time
  4. Take Turns: Players alternate submitting words for the same letter combination
  5. No Word Repeats: Words can only be used once during the ENTIRE game
  6. Keep Going: Players continue alternating until one player:
    • Runs out of time ‚è±Ô∏è
    • Can't think of a valid word (can retry invalid words)
  7. Win the Round: If your opponent fails, you win the round!
  8. ‚ö†Ô∏è Special Rule: The same letter combination (e.g., A‚ÜíT) cannot be reused in future rounds. If it's randomly selected again, players must choose new letters

üìú Word Requirements

  • Minimum Length: At least 3 letters
  • Maximum Length: No more than 15 letters
  • Valid Dictionary Words: Must be a real English word
  • Must Start: With the chosen starting letter
  • Must End: With the chosen ending letter
  • No Duplicates: Cannot be used more than once per game (especially important in Battle Royale!)

üí° Tips & Strategy

üéÆ Normal Mode Tips:
  • Speed is key - sometimes simple words win!
  • Think of multiple options quickly
  • Common letter combinations usually have many words
‚öîÔ∏è Battle Royale Mode Tips:
  • Remember ALL previously used words throughout the game
  • Start with simple words to maintain momentum
  • Save harder/longer words for later in the round
  • Watch the displayed words list to avoid repetition
  • Track which letter combinations have been used
  • Think ahead while your opponent is taking their turn
  • Don't panic when timer resets - you get full time each turn
üìö General Tips:

  • Practice makes perfect!
  • Letters like E, S, T, R are very versatile
